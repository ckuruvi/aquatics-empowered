Aquatic Levels Information

Level One: can facilitate any aquatic stretching, excercises, walking, or floating excercises and below.

Level Two: can facilitate novice swimming, walking, running, excercises and below.
Average aquatics level

Level Three: can facilitate intermediate swimming, running excercises and below.
Avid aquatics level

Level Four: can facilitate advanced swimming, running, excercises and below.
Profession athlete, or tri-athlete level
