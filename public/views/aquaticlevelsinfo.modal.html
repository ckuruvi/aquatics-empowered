Aquatic Levels Information

Level One: any aquatic stretching, excercises, walking, or floating excercises and below.

Level Two: novice swimming, walking, running, excercises and below.
Average aquatics level

Level Three: intermediate swimming, running excercises and below.
Avid aquatics level

Level Four: advanced swimming, running, excercises and below.
Profession athlete, or tri-athlete level
